# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

This is a personal golf-training guide for Darren: a right-handed skilled amateur working from high-80s scoring toward consistent rounds in the 70s by fixing a two-way miss. It will often be used on mobile web at the range, at home before practice, and between sessions as a quick reference.

## Product Purpose

The page turns a swing diagnosis into a calm, structured 30-day practice plan with drills, video references, filming guidance, progress tracking, and course strategy. Success means the next practice action is obvious, the mechanical goal stays clear, and the plan builds confidence around a repeatable stock shot.

## Brand Personality

Calm, exacting, grounded. The voice should feel like a patient coach and a useful practice notebook, not a sales page. The visual world should lean green and neutral, with complementary bursts used only when they clarify priority, status, warning, or encouragement.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing pages with glossy cards, heroic metrics, or vague productivity language.
- Golf hype, quick-fix claims, or "secret move" content that overpromises.
- Dense training manuals that feel punitive or hard to use on a phone.
- Decorative card overload, repeated section labels, and visual noise that slows practice flow.

## Design Principles

- Mobile first at the range: everything important should scan and tap cleanly on a phone in outdoor light.
- Calm confidence over intensity: reduce swing anxiety by making the next rep obvious.
- Practice is the interface: prioritize day, drill, duration, and feedback cues over decoration.
- Specific coaching beats generic polish: copy should say exactly what to do and what to look for.
- Small bursts, real hierarchy: use accent color, labels, and motion sparingly to mark meaningful changes.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

No special accessibility requirements beyond a strong default: WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-friendly interactive elements, clear focus states, reduced-motion support, and touch targets appropriate for mobile web.
